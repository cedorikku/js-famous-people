Bronny James is currently a rookie basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers, drafted 55th overall in the 2024 NBA draft. This makes him and his father, LeBron James Sr., the first ever father-son duo to be active teammates in the NBA.

Jeff Bezos is the founder of Amazon, one of the world's largest online retailers, and the former CEO of the company. He is also the founder of Blue Origin, a private aerospace manufacturer and sub-orbital spaceflight services company. As one of the wealthiest individuals in the world, Bezos has significantly impacted the e-commerce, technology, and space exploration industries.
